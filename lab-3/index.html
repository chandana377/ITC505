Serving Fonts — Lab 3

How I served the font (in my words)

I hosted two webfonts locally and used @font-face to load them: KomikaLocal for headings and BangersLocal for body text. The font files (woff2, woff, ttf fallbacks) are placed in fonts/ and referenced with relative URLs. Browsers request those font files when rendering the page, so visitors who don't have these fonts installed still see the custom fonts.

Why I used two fonts

I used two different hosted fonts to demonstrate serving multiple custom fonts and to make the difference obvious to graders: one font for headings/title (KomikaLocal) and another for body text and paragraph samples (BangersLocal).
